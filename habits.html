The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People



The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People by Stephen R. Covey is a guide to personal and professional effectiveness through principle-centered living. The book presents seven habits that help individuals move from dependence to independence and then to interdependence. The first three habits—Be Proactive, Begin with the End in Mind, and Put First Things First—focus on self-mastery and personal responsibility. The next three—Think Win-Win, Seek First to Understand, Then to Be Understood, and Synergize—emphasize effective relationships and collaboration. The final habit, Sharpen the Saw, encourages continuous renewal and self-care across physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual dimensions. Together, these habits foster a balanced, purposeful, and productive life.
